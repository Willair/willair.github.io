ABOUT

I would like to introduce Dewars Services Pty Ltd to you as a one stop cleaning company. With over 25 years in the cleaning industry, we have a perfect safety record in that time. We can offer a range of services which include

  • Window Cleaning – Commercial, High Rise and gantry work
  • High access EWP cleaning – Shopping centre cleans, external wash downs
  • Hot and cold water pressure cleaning
  • Tile deep cleaning
  • Glass and Stainless Steel nano treatments
  • Commercial Office Cleaning
  • Eco-Shield - high quality, proven cleaning chemicals are used and distributed

Over that period of time we have worked on some of the most sensitive sites with safety and environmental procedures in place such as Brisbane, Coolangatta and Townsville airports, as well as Military bases throughout Queensland.

We have also completed high cleans in numerous shopping centres, universities, schools and several high rises throughout Brisbane. All our staff have current Police Checks, EWP licences and working at heights training. Our company can give you peace of mind, in the knowledge that we are fully insured to $20M and all staff are employed by our business, and not just contractors.

With safety being our highest priority, we will not compromise on cleaning quality. Our guarantee is to return quotes quickly, turn up when booked in, and rectify any issues by the next working day.

I look forward to speaking with you about making all your cleaning needs totally Paneless! Yours faithfully,

Allan Dewar
Managing Director
