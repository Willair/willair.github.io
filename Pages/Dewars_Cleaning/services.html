Carpet Cleaning

Generally, there are two methods of carpet cleaning - the extraction method and encapsulation method. We have both types of equipment to cover all your carpet needs. Encapsulation is a very quiet cleaning method, which leaves carpets quite dry soon after cleaning.

Without a well programmed carpet cleaning schedule, the carpet will become dirty and lifeless which leads to a shorter life span. With each carpet clean, an amount of dirt is extracted. If this dirt is walked in by constant foot traffic, the carpet will wear prematurely and need to be replaced earlier.

Window Cleaning

This is the most cost-effective way to maintain the glass façade of your building. Pollutants in the air from traffic and industry settle on the exterior of buildings, causing paint to age and glass to etch, and restoring damaged glass is very expensive.

Investing in a well scheduled glass cleaning programme will ensure the glass will not only look cleaner but give it a longer lifespan and lower long-term maintenance cost – an important factor due to most new eco-friendly buildings being 90% glass.

Clean windows have a positive effect with staff atmosphere and workplace ambience.
